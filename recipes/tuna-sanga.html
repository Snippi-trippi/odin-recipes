Tuna Sandwich Recipe

Here is the almighty Tuna Sanga

Some believe that by eating the tuna sanga, you can obtain immortality

So if you make one, jump off a cliff and find out the truth to above

Ingredients

  • Tuna (preferably straight from the mutt)
  • Sandwich
  • Mayonaise (straight from the ol' cock n' balls)
  • Imagination
  • Tears from a gypsy

Steps

  1. Lick the tuna from the mutt and spit it out into a bowl
  2. Rub out a quick one and finish in the bowl
  3. Finally, add imagination and tears from a gypsy to the bowl and use the sandwich to become an idiot sandwich
